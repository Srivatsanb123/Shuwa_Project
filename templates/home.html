Sign Language Translator and Trainer

The primary purpose of Shuwa is to translate sign language into the language that is desired. This allows deaf people to communicate more easily with people who do not understand sign language and vice versa. This application helps bridge the communication gap between the deaf and hearing communities.

Additionally, you can develop the basic skills that you need in order to effectively communicate with people who have hearing disabilities by learning the basics of sign language. You can also be familiar with the different tools that are available to assist people with hearing disabilities, such as hearing aids and captioning services.
